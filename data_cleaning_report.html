04 — Key Insights
📈 Trend

Experience Drives Pay (r ≈ 0.55)

A moderate positive correlation exists between years of experience and salary. Engineering commands the steepest slope — each additional year yields ~$2.8K more than the company average.

💡 Insight

Low Performers Log More Overtime

Heatmap analysis reveals that Low-performance employees average 6.8 overtime hours/week vs 4.2 for Excellent performers — suggesting inefficiency rather than dedication.

⚡ Action

HR Has Lowest Satisfaction (5.2)

HR department scores 0.6 points below average satisfaction despite near-average salaries. This decoupling suggests non-monetary drivers (autonomy, growth paths) need review.

💡 Insight

Salary Spread Widest in Engineering

Violin plots show Engineering has the broadest interquartile salary range (~$45K–$105K), reflecting seniority tiers. Finance is the most compressed, suggesting flatter comp bands.
